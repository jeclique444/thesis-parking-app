# iParkBayan Design Ideas

## Context
Smart Parking Space Detection and Reservation System for Lipa City Downtown.
App name: iParkBayan. Users: Drivers (mobile app) + Administrators (dashboard).

## Idea 1: Urban Tech Brutalism
**Design Movement:** Digital Brutalism meets Urban Infrastructure
**Core Principles:**
- Raw, functional aesthetics with deliberate imperfections
- High-contrast monochrome base with electric accent pops
- Data-first layout: information hierarchy over decoration
- Grid-based slot visualization as primary visual motif

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0D0D0D) background with electric lime (#B5FF3C) as the primary action color, evoking traffic signals and urban signage. White for data, red/green for slot status.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric split-screen — left panel for map/grid, right panel for actions. No centered hero sections.

**Signature Elements:**
- Parking grid rendered as bold monospace cells
- Thick border dividers and exposed structural lines
- Status indicators styled like traffic lights

**Interaction Philosophy:** Immediate feedback, no transitions — clicks snap, states toggle instantly.

**Animation:** Minimal — only status change flashes and slot selection pulses.

**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (headings, bold) + JetBrains Mono (data/status) — technical and raw.

## Idea 2: Civic Tech — Clean Filipino Urban Identity ✅ SELECTED
**Design Movement:** Modern Civic Technology / Material Design 3 with Filipino warmth
**Core Principles:**
- Trustworthy and approachable — government-grade clarity with consumer-grade polish
- Deep navy blue as authority color, paired with warm amber/gold for action
- Card-based layout with generous whitespace and soft depth
- Mobile-first: every screen designed as a phone screen first

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0F2B5B) as primary brand color — evoking trust, authority, and the Philippine flag. Warm amber (#F59E0B) for CTAs and available slots (warmth, action). Emerald green for available, rose red for occupied. Soft off-white (#F8FAFC) background.

**Layout Paradigm:** Bottom-navigation mobile shell with persistent header. Dashboard uses left sidebar with content area. Parking grid uses a visual floor-plan style layout.

**Signature Elements:**
- Parking slot grid with color-coded cells (green/red/amber)
- Floating action button for quick reservation
- Subtle wave/curve dividers referencing Philippine coastal geography

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, guided flows — reservation is a multi-step wizard. Feedback is warm and encouraging.

**Animation:** Framer Motion slide-up transitions, slot pulse animations for real-time updates, skeleton loaders.

**Typography System:** Plus Jakarta Sans (headings — modern Filipino tech feel) + Inter (body — readable) with weight contrast between display and body text.

## Idea 3: Glassmorphism Night City
**Design Movement:** Glassmorphism / Cyberpunk Lite
**Core Principles:**
- Dark city night aesthetic with glowing neon accents
- Frosted glass cards over dark gradient backgrounds
- Immersive full-screen map as background layer
- Neon green/cyan for available, neon red for occupied

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#111827) background with cyan (#06B6D4) and neon green (#22C55E) accents. Frosted panels at 20% opacity white. Creates a "command center" feel.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-screen map background with floating overlay panels. Parking slots rendered as glowing grid cells.

**Signature Elements:**
- Frosted glass cards with backdrop-blur
- Glowing border effects on selected slots
- Animated particle background suggesting city movement

**Interaction Philosophy:** Immersive and cinematic — every interaction feels like operating a high-tech system.

**Animation:** Glow pulse on available slots, glass panel slide-ins, neon flicker on status changes.

**Typography System:** Outfit (headings — futuristic clean) + Roboto Mono (data) — sci-fi command center feel.
